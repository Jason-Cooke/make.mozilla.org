Content Guidelines

This document describes the various types of content that is inappropriate on the Events Platform. If you are a user of the Events Platform, you must not provide or upload content that violates these guidelines. We may update this list from time to time.

  • 1. No obscene content or graphic depictions of sexuality or violence.
  • 2. No content that infringes anyone’s rights, including intellectual property or other proprietary rights or rights or privacy or personhood.
  • 3. No content that is designed to harm Mozilla or users (such as malicious code, viruses, spyware or malware).
  • 4. No content that is illegal or promotes illegal activities, including content that is deceptive, misleading, fraudulent or is designed to phish or perform other identity theft.
  • 5. No content that glamorizes or encourages alcohol, drug or tobacco use.
  • 6. No content that promotes gambling.
  • 7. No content that engages in the advertisement of illegal or controlled products or services.
  • 8. No content that is deceptive or fraudulent.
  • 9. No content that is harmful to minors.
  • 10. No content or events that are un-related in anyway to Mozilla’s Webmaker Initiative.
  • 11. No content that is hate speech or degrades, intimidates, incites violence against, or encourages prejudicial action against someone or a group based on age, gender, race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, sexual orientation, disability, region, geographic location or other protected category.
